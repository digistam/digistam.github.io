Snow

How does it work ?

      Create a Notepad file (readme.txt)
      Write a long line with the hyphen key
      ----------------------------------
      Store readme.txt in c:\magic folder
      Download snow.exea
      Store snow.exe in c:\magic folder
      Open dosbox cmd
      Cd c:\magic
      Command: "snow -C -m "My swiss bank account number is 45656684512263" -p "magic" readme.txt readme2.txt
      Command: "snow -C -p "magic" readme2.txt" -> will show the hidden text
      Open readme.txt in Notepad and press CTRL+A and you will see one short line of blue shaded content
      Open readme2.txt in Notepad and press CTRL+A and you will a larger selection of blue shaded content
      The data inside readme2.txt is hidden in the form of spaces and tabs
